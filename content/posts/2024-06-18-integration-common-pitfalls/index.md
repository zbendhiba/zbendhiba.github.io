3 mistakes that are preventing you from overcoming integration challenges:

1. Too Much Protocol Focus
You spend excessive time coding access to various protocols rather than utilizing existing solutions, focusing more on implementation than on quickly delivering business value.

2. Lack of Flexibility
Your integration design isn't adaptable, making it difficult to introduce updates or new steps efficiently.

3. Poor Monitoring of Data Flows
Without a clear integration strategy, overseeing and managing the data flows within your system becomes complicated, making it tough to adapt to system evolutions.
